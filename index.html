Sound design isn't a boring job about plugins.

It's a craft where you build atmosphere, emotion and entire worlds through sound. I teach people to hear the world differently — and after one month you'll sound-design a scene from your favourite film on your own.

  1. Choosing a DAW — the pros & cons of each
  2. Training your ear to hear sound that isn't there yet
  3. How to stylize sound
  4. How to properly approach a project
  5. How to think like a sound designer
  6. The technical side of achieving the right sound
  7. Headphone types & how to choose the right ones
  8. Audio gear overview & what it's used for
  9. Real confidence working with sound overall
  10. Building a portfolio tailored to you
  11. A personalized approach
  12. Working with music & voice (processing, not composing)
  13. Plugin types, what they do & my go-to tools
  14. Mixing & mastering
